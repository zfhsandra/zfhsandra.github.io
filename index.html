About

My research focuses on intelligent spatial analysis and modeling under resource constraints: (1) sample design under budget limits, (2) spatial modeling when data are scarce, and (3) efficient implementations under constrained computing resources.
The proposed methods are applied in the field of digital soil mapping, groundwater level change prediction, and vegetation type inference.

本人研究围绕资源受限情景下的智能空间分析与建模展开，包括（1）预算受限下的高效布样， （2）数据稀缺条件下的空间建模，（3）算力约束下的高效实现。应用方向涵盖数字土壤制图、地下水埋深变化预测与植被类型推测。
